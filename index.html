  • About Me

    Who am I?

    I'm Yu-Kuan, or Cliff, currently a graduate student at the School of Information at the University of Texas at Austin, aiming to be a UX designer. As I have my bachelor's in psychology, I hope to contribute to the field by combining my knowledge in human behavior, perception, and cognition with the UX skillsets I'm learning right now.

    My interest in UX began when I took Design Psychology in my senior year. The class emphasized on how designs can influence the human mind and thus increase work efficiency and reduce human error, and I became intrigued in the concept.

    Logo Design Concept

    My full name is Yu-Kuan Hwang, so I took each of the captialized letter into account when I created my logo. Instantly, anyone can see the letters Y and K. My last name's first letter, H, on the contrary, is placed in the middle, using the Gestalt's principle of closure, which people see complete figures even when part of the information is missing.
